Commitment to efficiency and quality.

We created Aluminca SRL in 1990 for aluminum die casting. Absolute accuracy at our work allows us not only to market our products from Rosario to the rest of the country but also to be recognized for our high quality nowadays.

At Aluminca SRL At Aluminca SRL we’ve been working with our customers and suppliers for more than 25 years, meeting the most demanding needs. We have a melting capacity over 20 ton and our own line of SEYS lighting products as well as a specialized area in 3D Design.
